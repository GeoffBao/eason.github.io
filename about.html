Education

  • 2016-2017: National Taiwan University

    I received one year of training in Mathematics and Physics at National Taiwan University. During my time at NTU, I completed a senior project in Modern Web Development and took several graduate courses, including General Relativity and Classical Electrodynamics. My academic performance ranked in the top three of the entire department.

    However, I discovered that my interests extended beyond Mathematics and Physics. I wanted more time for self-exploration and learning in other fields, and NTU's rigid course requirements couldn't satisfy my diverse learning needs and desires. As a result, I made the decision to voluntarily drop out after finishing my freshman year in 2017. It turned out to be one of the best decisions I've made in my life.

  • 2019-2020: Minerva University

    After two years of self-exploration, extensive reading, and doing some software contract work, I attended Minerva University to learn how to apply cross-disciplinary mental models in a real-world setting. I majored in Computational Science, but decided to voluntarily drop out after finishing my freshman year (2020) to start my startup.
