How to Download This Template

At Downshift, we’ve been thinking about how we approach year-end reflection. Every December, we're bombarded with messages about doing more, achieving more, and becoming more—as if who we are isn’t already enough.

This time of year also brings the familiar rituals of New Year’s resolutions and goal-setting—well-meaning promises we make to ourselves. Yet so often, they stem from a sense of lack: what we believe is missing, broken, or not good enough. Rarely do they honor the gifts, values, and qualities we already possess.

The truth is, most of us high performers have spent years perfecting the art of achievement. We know how to set goals, crush targets, and push boundaries. But what we haven’t learned is how to find peace in who we are, regardless of what we accomplish. It’s a particular kind of exhaustion—one that comes from constantly trying to prove our worth through our work.

That’s why we created Downshift’s Annual Review. It’s not about adding more to your plate or finding new ways to optimize yourself. Instead, it’s an invitation to pause, reconnect with who you are beneath the achievements, and discover what emerges when you lead from a place of wholeness rather than constant striving.

This is a space to reflect, not to fix. To align with what matters, not to hustle for what’s next. And we think you’ll be pleasantly surprised by what emerges and unfolds when you connect to your essence—a sense of clarity, alignment, or even peace might begin to take shape in ways you didn’t expect.

If this resonates, we invite you to approach your annual reflection differently this year. No pressure, no hustle—just an opportunity to explore what might be possible when you start from “being” rather than “doing.”

The Downshift Team

Our Annual Review is more than a reflection—it’s an invitation to come home to yourself. Through this eight-step journey, you’ll explore where you’ve been, reconnect with what matters most, and uncover what’s possible when you live a life rooted in your essence.

Each step and exercise has been thoughtfully crafted to guide you inward, helping you slow down, reflect, and realign. Take your time. There’s no rush—just an opportunity to step into the new year with greater clarity, presence, and intention.

If you have any questions, reflections, or feedback along the way, we’d love to hear from you. Feel free to reach out anytime at hello@downshift.me.
